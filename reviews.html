The Persean Chronicles - Music Review

"MesoTroniK did the ambient music for one of the quests in Persean Chronicles (get it today!). He was fair, responsive, professional, and delivered in a timely manner (faster than I expected, actually, since I had no deadline for it). When I gave feedback (I requested some more droning in the lower register), he listened, experimented, and came up with something completely awesome. It feels like you're underwater, passing by reaching algae and distant sunbeams, danger lurking below and around. I believe that this gives the quest a creepy, slightly unsettling ambience that you can't get from text alone. Highly recommend. I will be shopping here for any future sound/music needs."

Wispbourne - Coder and Starsector Modder
